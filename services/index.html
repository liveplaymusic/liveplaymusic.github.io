Services for Individuals with Special Needs

 

Nicole Kowalewski, BMT, MTA is the Bow Valley’s only Accredited Music Therapist. She has experience working with individuals of all ages. She has worked with kids with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, ADHD, Asperger’s, and other cognitive and physical disabilities. She has also worked extensively with seniors with dementia and Alzheimer’s Disease in individual and group settings. She currently runs the music therapy program at Scenic Acres Retirement Residence. She is available to take on more individual clients.

 

For more information about music therapy please visit www.musictherapy.ca

 

 

Adapted Music Lessons

Adapted Music Lessons are available for individuals with special needs whose goal is to learn to play a musical instrument and learn the fundamentals of music. Adapted lessons in piano, guitar and voice are available for all ages and abilities living in Canmore or Banff.

 

 

Music Therapy (Individual Sessions)

Music Therapy is different from adapted music lessons because of its goals. Music therapy utilizes music and musical activities in ways that work towards a variety of non-musical goals such as increasing self-esteem, socialization and attention span or decreasing anxiety, aggressive behaviour or depression for example. Learning to play an instrument is not a goal of music therapy, however it may occur as a result of the music therapy process.

 

 

Music Therapy (Group Sessions)

If you are part of an organization or facility that offers services to people who would benefit from a music therapy group, please contact us about the possibility of starting a music therapy program.
