About Our Teachers

 

Nicole Kowalewski has a Bachelor of Music Therapy from Acadia University where she studied voice and piano. Nicole is an accredited music therapist as well as a teacher. She also plays the guitar.

 

Laurie Tepper has a Bachelor of Music from the University of Winnipeg. She has been teaching piano lessons in Canmore since 2007. She also offers music theory classes.

 

 

Piano Lessons

We accept students ages 4 and up. Adult beginners are welcome and flexible lesson schedules are available to fit a busy work/life schedule. Lessons run from September to June with no lessons on school or stat holidays. Whether you want to learn to play for your own enjoyment or you want to prepare for a conservatory exam we will help you reach your goals. We follow the Faber and Faber series of books with the option to advance to the Canadian Conservatory grade levels or continue playing your favourite genre just for fun.

​We hold two recitals each year for students to participate in. One is just before the Christmas Break and one is mid-June. This allows parents to see their child’s progress over the course of the year and gives the students invaluable performance experience. The recitals are for piano and voice students. There is no fee for the recitals.

Voice Lessons

We accept students ages 6 and up. Adult beginners are welcome and flexible lessons schedules are available to fit a busy work/life schedule. Lessons run from September to June with no lessons on school or stat holidays. Whether you want to sing for your own enjoyment or you want to prepare for a conservatory voice exam we will help you reach your goals. We use the Conservatory Canada Voice books and a variety of Musical Theatre, Classical and Pop books to introduce students to a broad range of styles.

Theory Lessons

Available as individual lessons or in small groups depending on demand.
