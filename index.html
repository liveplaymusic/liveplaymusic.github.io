Welcome!

Our goal is to nurture an enjoyment for music that leads to self-motivated learning. We believe piano is an invaluable foundation to any musical education and transferable when learning different instruments  Everyone has the potential to discover their musical abilities and our job is to help build the confidence to let those talents shine through!

 

 

Piano Lessons

We accept students ages 4 and up. Adult beginners are welcome and flexible lesson schedules are available to fit a busy work/life schedule.

Voice Lessons

Adapted Music Lessons

Adapted Music Lessons are available for individuals with special needs whose goal is to learn to play a musical instrument and learn the fundamentals of music. Adapted lessons in piano, guitar and voice are available for all ages and abilities living in Canmore or Banff.
 
Click to Learn More

Music Therapy (Individual Sessions)

Music therapy utilizes music and musical activities in ways that work towards a variety of non-musical goals such as increasing self-esteem, socialization and attention span or decreasing anxiety, aggressive behaviour or depression.
 
Click to Learn More

Music Therapy (Group Sessions)

If you are part of an organization or facility that offers services to people who would benefit from a music therapy group, please contact us about the possibility of starting a music therapy program.
